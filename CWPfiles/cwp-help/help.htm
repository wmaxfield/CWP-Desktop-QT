Getting Started

Please note that after selecting a Bible, this help screen will no longer appear. To get back to this screen, choose "Show Help" from the Help menu

Everything is "clickable."  If the English Dictionary option is selected under preferences, clicking on a word shows its definition from the Webster's dictionary.

If a word is colored then clicking on the word will bring up the original language definition according to Strong's.

If a verse reference is colored, then clicking on that verse reference will take you to that verse in the Bible window.  This works for the Prophecy Trail, and for the Smith's Bible Dictionary. 

The Smith's Bible dictionary contains images scanned from an original 100+ year old book.  The text was OCR read, and corrected by hand.  The verse references were computer processed, so some errors in verse navigation still exist.

All Bibles used in CWP and all all other text or pictures are Public Domain, or used with permission from the authors.

Selecting and Viewing Bibles

 

1.      click the (Bibles Button).

2.      Select the translation(s) you wish to view from the resulting Choose Bibles list. When finished, click "OK"

Note: If more than one Bible is selected, they will be displayed side by side (in the order they are selected). Only the first Bible in the display is searched, the left-most Bible.

 

3.      To remove a translation from the Selected Bibles list, click the  button and click on the Bible(s) you wish to remove from the Selected Bibles List. The Bible will be moved to the Available Bibles list. When finished, click the OK button.

 

Navigating a Bible

 

1.      Click the in the Toolbar.

2.      Select the desired Book from the Select Book In Bible Window .

 

     3.      Select the desired Chapter from the Select Chapter List .

 

    

Searching a Bible

 

1.      Click the in the Toolbar.

2.      Type in the word or phrase you are looking for, press Enter to start the search or click on the search button in the window.

3.      To bring up a particular verse of interest, simply click on it in the displayed list. It will be immediately shown in the Bible display windows.  If your desired search result is not in the list, click the Search button again.  The search is limited to about 100 items each time.  Search is completely done when the "From Bookname to Bookname" read the same.  (From Genesis to Genesis means the entire Bible has been searched.)

3.      To Clear the current search, and start over, click click the Clear button  .

Note: The search window is also scrollable (when applicable). Also, the search engine only searches the first (left most Bible) on screen.

 

 

Using the Strong’s Concordance

 

1.      Open a Bible translation that supports the integrated Strong’s Concordance e.g. cKJV,  WEB, etc.

2.      Click the particular word you wish to see the Greek (NT), or Hebrew (OT) Strong’s definition for. This only applies to the words of a different color; from inside the Strong’s window (pictured below), you can touch any word that is a different color to be taken to the word's Strong's definition.

Creating a Bookmark

 

1.      Touch the B next to the verse you wish to bookmark.  ("Show Bookmark Tag" must be enabled in Preferences.)

2.      Select the desired topic from the Book Mark Selector screen, and then touch the Add  verse to selected Topic button; or to create a Topic, touch the New Topic button, enter the new topic and then touch the Add Bookmark button.  The Topics are shown in a "rotating wheel" selector.  Just click and drag the wheel to move to the next topic under the magnifier lens.
